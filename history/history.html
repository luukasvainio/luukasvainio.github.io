Founding of the Club

Inter Mifflin FC was founded in the spring of 2023 by a group of college athlete swimmers at Dunder Mifflin University. Seeking to venture beyond the confines of the pool, they yearned for a new challenge. Destiny itself seemed to intervene, uniting them with top soccer prospects to create a team destined for greatness. Thus, Inter Mifflin FC was born, ready to conquer the intramural soccer league.

Milestones and Achievements

Although this is our first season, Inter Mifflin FC has already triumphed in every aspect off the field. Our team has built a strong foundation of camaraderie, strategic planning, and unwavering determination. We have won the hearts of our peers and established a reputation for excellence. As we eagerly await the start of the intramural season, we are confident that victory is within our grasp.

Our Mission and Values

Inter Mifflin FC is driven by a mission to embody the spirit of champions. Chosen by destiny, we never back down from a challenge. We go all out in everything we do, showing no mercy to our opponents. Our values are rooted in perseverance, teamwork, and an unyielding commitment to excellence. We strive to inspire others to embrace the thrill of competition and the joy of sportsmanship.
